Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. You may contact us at any time using the contact page.

7. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the contact page.
